# Startup Cost Breakdown - $1,000 Request

## Use of Funds

**Clarification**: "Hosting" = All tech costs (infrastructure, services, tools)

### 1. Business Formation Costs: ~$500-800

#### LLC Formation (Recommended)
- **State Filing Fee**: $50-500 (varies by state)
  - Delaware: $90
  - Wyoming: $60
  - Nevada: $75
  - California: $70 + $800 annual franchise tax
  - Texas: $300
  - New York: $200
  - Most states: $50-150

- **Registered Agent Service**: $50-300/year
  - Required in most states
  - Can use yourself if you have physical address in state
  - Professional service: $50-150/year

- **Operating Agreement**: $100-200
  - Legal document (can use template, but legal review recommended)
  - Attorney review: $200-500

- **Business License** (if required): $50-200
  - Varies by location and business type
  - Some states/cities require for online businesses

- **Legal Consultation** (one-time): $200-500
  - Operating agreement review
  - Tax structure advice
  - Compliance guidance

**Total Business Formation**: $450-1,700 (varies significantly by state and services)

#### Corporation Formation (Alternative)
- **State Filing Fee**: $50-500
- **Registered Agent**: $50-300/year
- **Articles of Incorporation**: $100-200
- **Bylaws**: $100-200
- **Legal Consultation**: $300-500

**Total**: $600-1,700

#### Recommended: DigitalOcean or Budget Cloud Option

- **Scaling**: $200-500/month as traffic grows

## Cost Optimization Recommendations

### Business Formation Savings
1. **Choose budget-friendly state**: Wyoming ($60) or Delaware ($90)
2. **Use yourself as registered agent** (if you have state address)
3. **Use online formation service**: $0-200 (vs. attorney $500+)
4. **Template operating agreement**: $0-50 (vs. attorney $200-500)

**Potential Savings**: $300-700

### Tech Costs Savings
1. **Start with DigitalOcean**: $64/month vs. $200-500 (includes all services)
2. **Self-host initially**: $10-20/month (if technically capable)
3. **Use free tiers**: AWS/GCP free credits for first year
4. **Bundle services**: Use managed services that include multiple components
5. **Open source tools**: Use free/open-source monitoring/logging tools
6. **Scale gradually**: Start small, upgrade as revenue grows

**Potential Savings**: $100-400/month

## Month 2+ Funding Gap

**Critical Concern**: $1,000 only covers Month 1 tech costs.

**Month 2+ Required**:
- Tech costs (hosting + services): $64-500/month
- Legal/compliance: $200-500 (one-time, but ongoing compliance)
- Insurance: $100-300/month

- Payment processing fees: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction

**Total Month 2+**: $864-3,300/month minimum

## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Revenue Risks

**Risk**: Lower transaction rates than projected  
**Mitigation**: 
- Focus on marketplace activation
- Improve matching quality
- Incentivize first transactions

**Risk**: Lower average transaction value  
**Mitigation**:
- Value-based pricing guidance
- Bundle products + services
- Premium listings

### Cost Risks

**Risk**: Tech costs scale faster than revenue  
**Mitigation**:
- Optimize infrastructure
- Use cost-effective providers (DigitalOcean)
- Scale gradually

**Risk**: Marketing CAC higher than projected  
**Mitigation**:
- Focus on organic channels
- Optimize conversion funnel
- Pause paid ads if CAC too high

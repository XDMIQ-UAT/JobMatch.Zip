## Key Growth Mechanisms

### 1. Organic Growth (40% of users)
- Content marketing + SEO
- Community building
- Word of mouth

### 2. Partnership-Driven (30% of users)
- LLC associations (10-20 partners)
- AI tool communities (5-10 partners)
- Business networks (5-10 partners)

### 3. Referral Program (20% of users)
- Viral referral mechanism
- Platform credits as rewards
- One-click invite system

### 4. Paid Acquisition (10% of users, Year 2+)
- Targeted advertising
- Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads
- Scales with revenue
